Campus Life

Indoor Sports Room

A huge room where students can play indoor sports like Chess, Carrom,etc. It also hosted all the indoor sports of Elpro Sports Fest-2019. The best part about it is that it is air-conditioned.

Football Field

It is a huge turf where students can play various sports such as Football or Cricket. It played a major part in the Elpro Sports Fest-2019 when it hosted all the interschool Football matches.

Chemistry Lab

A lab where students can perform various experiments related to Chemistry or Physics under adult supervision. Students of higher grades can learn and explore about various things.

What's New?

  • New Long Jump Pit and Running Track

    A Olympic-sized long jump pit and a 100 m running track. After building it, our school also got the honour to host the interschool Sports Fest. Before this, places where running races could be held were limited. But after it was built, there is no difficulty in hosting such events.

  • New School Auditorium

    A huge 8000 sq. ft auditorium where events such sdthe Solo Singing Competion-2019, Elpro Sports Fest-2019, Group Singing Competion-2019, etc. where held. Our Annual Day Function was also held here.

  • New Multisport Rooftop Facility

    The Multisport Rooftop Facility, where various sports such as Football, Cricket, Footcricket, etc. can be played. Before this was built, students hardly had any place to play. Our Inter-house competitions are also held in this Multisport Rooftop Facility.
